About Me

I'm a web developer and designer based in Pakistan, working across frontend development, web design, and everyday office & technical work. My approach is simple: understand the problem, then build it properly.

Alongside web development, I also handle document, spreadsheet, and presentation work using MS Office, and hold a technical diploma in tube well operation — a mix of digital and hands-on skills.

Over the years I've worked with individuals, small businesses, and startups — turning rough ideas into finished, working websites. I care about the small details: how a page feels to scroll through, how fast it loads, how clearly the content is organized, and how well it works on a phone versus a desktop.

I believe good work comes from listening first. Before writing a single line of code, I try to understand what the client actually needs — not just what they ask for. That means asking questions, suggesting better approaches when I see one, and being honest about timelines and tradeoffs.

Outside of pure development, I'm comfortable handling the practical side of projects too — proposals, documentation, spreadsheets, and presentations — so clients get a complete package, not just code.

Frontend Development UI

Frontend development is the art of building everything a user sees and interacts with on a website. It combines structure, style, and behavior to create fast, responsive, and beautiful web experiences.

HTML

The backbone of every website. HTML defines the structure and content of web pages — headings, paragraphs, images, links, forms, and more.

CSS

CSS controls the visual presentation — colors, layouts, fonts, spacing, animations, and responsive design for any screen size.

JavaScript

JavaScript adds interactivity and logic — from dropdown menus to complex web applications with real-time updates.
